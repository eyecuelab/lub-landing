Everyone is different
Get the opinions that matter

Looking for a new place to dine is a common struggle. Unfortunately, visiting a normal opinion aggregation platform allows you to only see an average of how everybody feels - including people you don't agree with.

LUB changes that by only presentinging you with recommendations from people like you.

Interesting, how does LUB accomplish that?

Likes and dislikes
We want to know them all

Simply let LUB know how you feel about a restaurant, a bar, or even a spa after you've visited there, and LUB will start to understand your palate and preferences.

Like a good personal assistant and a great friend, LUB will get to know you better as you share more with LUB.

Ok... but how does that help to generate personal recommendations?

Smart recommendations
Only from people like you

When you share opinions on LUB, LUB uses data science to identify other people in the community who share your preferences.

From there, LUB predicts how you might enjoy a new experience, based on your personal taste and the collective sentiment of people who think and feel like you.

Got it. So is LUB just a food and drink recommendation app?

LUB is a universal
opinion platform
with infinite possibilities

LUB continues to evolve with every opinion you leave. As it gets to know you better, it also gains a better understanding of everyone using the app.

Fresh questions are delivered regularly, guaranteeing that LUB won't lose track of what you like, or put you in a rut. As the community around LUB grows, everyone benefits from eachother's opinions.
